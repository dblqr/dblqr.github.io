How It Works

  1. Enter up to 3 friend codes by typing them in, scanning QR codes, or uploading a text file.
  2. The app generates a time-stamped QR code for each friend code that can be scanned in-game.
  3. QR codes auto-refresh every 10 seconds to stay valid for the game.
  4. Share your codes with friends by exporting to a .txt file or letting them scan your screen.
